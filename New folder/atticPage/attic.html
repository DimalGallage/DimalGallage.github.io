Attic is an android mobile application which use Augmented reality and Machine learning technologies to faciliate online furniture purchasing.

This application allows users to view 3D models of furniture items provided by manufacturers in their environment after analysing users background with data science.

What is the problem?

In the current world most of the items we see in the market today can be purchased through e-commerce applications and e-stores but when we consider furniture industry most people would be reluctant to make a purchase over internet due to several factors.
Unless people are familiar ans super confident with the manufacturer and their products, they wouldn't make such a big purchase over internet.

So the customers go to showrooms, but even in the showrooms customers have no way on deciding on a product which match their interior.

Here's our solution

To Address above problems we came up with aa solution, a mobile applicaion which can analyse user backgroung through machine learning and display most appropriate furniture product in the market through augmented reality display.
