A Product Design Case Study

Tippy

Tippy is a disposable, biodegradable fingertip cover to ensure public health safety at public touch-screens,ATMs and vending machines.

01.

Problem Background Research


People are much concerned about their health at public places more than ever, therefore people started using precautions such as facemasks to minimize the spread of germs. Also, to minimize contacts with others more and more individuals tend to use public automated services such as ATMs, Food ordering KIOSK , vending machines or automated fingerprint scanning machines. But the problem is the higher the number of users in such places, higher the probability of spreading germs through screen surfaces or buttons.

02.

The Solution


Since when using the above-mentioned public touchscreens or vending machines an individual only uses their fingertips. Therefore, we only need to contain the spread of bacteria from one’s fingertip to the screen or the button.

The solution designed for the above problem is a disposable latex fingertip cover.

03.

The Design


The design of this cover is shaped to represent the shape of actual top of the finger. The flat design minimizes the space taken by a single unit and maximize the use of storage space.

The thick brim around the opening of the cover allows users to easily open the cover and wear t on the fingertip.

04.

Use of Material


The material used for the fingertip cover is same as that of latex surgical gloves. The reason for such selection is because of its elastic properties allows the proper interactions with touch screens and fingerprint scanners through the material.

Also, its biodegradable properties minimize the effect on environmental pollution. Therefore, this product can be considered environmentally friendly.

05.

Why only the fingertip?


Since a person interacts with touchscreens or vending machine buttons with only their fingertips. The protection should only over the surface on contact.

The alternative solution would be using a normal disposable glove which is a huge wase of material & money.
