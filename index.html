Frequently Asked Questions

Why should I migrate my website to Webflow or Framer?

Webflow and Framer offer faster performance, greater design flexibility, powerful CMS features, and fewer dependencies on plugins. That means less maintenance, more creative freedom, and a site that scales with your business.

What platforms can you migrate from?

We migrate websites from WordPress, Wix, Squarespace, Shopify, custom-coded platforms, and more. Our process ensures a smooth transition with no loss of design quality, SEO value, or content.

How long does a typical migration take?

Timelines depend on the size and complexity of your site. Smaller websites can take 2–3 weeks, while larger or highly customized projects may take 4–6 weeks. We’ll provide a clear timeline during your free migration audit.

Do you also handle design, or just development?

We do both. Our team includes experienced designers who can refresh your brand and UI if needed, or we can migrate your existing design pixel-perfect into Webflow/Framer.

What if my team wants to manage the site after launch?

We provide hands-on Webflow training for your team, along with documentation and ongoing support options. You’ll have full control without relying on developers for every small change.

Do you offer support after the migration is complete?

Yes. We provide reliable aftercare packages that include ongoing support, security monitoring, and performance optimization to keep your site in top shape.

Can you also help with conversions and marketing strategy?

Absolutely. Our builds are designed for growth — from conversion-focused layouts to marketing tool integrations. Your website won’t just look good, it will actively help generate leads and sales.
